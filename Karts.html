Karts and Krafts
A couch play kart racing game where you decide how you win.
Either win the race fair and square by out-driving your competition and using
powerups to your advantage
or
Annihilate your competition with various weapons to be the last kart driving!


In Karts and Krafts I was the only programmer on the team so all of the code
in the game was written by me.
The project was developed for about 6 months over my 2nd year at university.

Most of my time on the project was spent on the racing AI which utilises a simple
Utility function to create different behaviours so that some of the AI will
try to collect weapons to attack the players, some will try to collect powerups
to get ahead and some will just try to race as fast as possible.
