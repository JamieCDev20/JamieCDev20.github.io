Infinite Bounty is an online 3rd person smash & grab shooter that recruits you and up
to 3 colleagues into the Galaxy's Number-1 inter-dimensional resource-acquisitions company:
Infinite Bounty.

Infinite Bounty is the project I am currently developing with my team for our
3rd year at university. I was one of two programmers on this project.
I Developed most of the front end systems such as the UI programming and
the player controller. I also worked on the networking for the game and the
AI programming.
